Free monthly street fair on Beacon Hill organized by Beacon Arts. Held on
the 2nd Saturday of each month from 11am–5pm, May through September, at
Roberto Maestas Festival Street + Beacon Hill Light Rail Plaza + Plaza
Roberto Maestas.

2026 dates: May 9, Jun 13, Jul 11, Aug 8, Sep 12. Each fair has a theme
(Celebrate Youth, Beacon Hill Pride, Garden Party, Animal Friends, Japan
on Beacon Hill).

Site is built on Wix; no ICS feed, RSS, Eventbrite organizer, or public
events API was discoverable. The schedule is a regular monthly cadence
with a fixed seasonal window, so it is well-suited to a recurring entry
under `sources/recurring/` rather than a custom Wix scraper.
