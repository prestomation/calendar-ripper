Crowdsourced community literary events calendar run by Seattle City of Literature
(a UNESCO Creative City of Literature designation holder). Supported by 4Culture
and the City of Seattle Office of Arts and Culture.

**Tockify calendar ID:** `scolcalendar`
**ICS feed:** `https://tockify.com/api/feeds/ics/scolcalendar`

115 upcoming events (2026-05-15) including author talks, book clubs, poetry nights,
book launches, silent book clubs, and readings at venues across Seattle (SPL
branches, Hugo House, Folio, bookstores, coffee shops, etc.).
