Trivia Night with Rich Burton

Starting on Thursday May 14th 2026

Monthly on every second Thursday

Join us for our monthly trivia night Downstairs at a/stir! How is this trivia different than every other trivia night, you ask? Well, over the two hour event, there will definitely be questions on random facts - ranging from pop culture to sportsball to music and movies to history to geography – but all with an added twist. 

Borrowing mechanics from classic and modern game shows and board games, we ramp up the fun by twisting each category into something new. Examples include: pushing your luck by earning double points on the categories in which you’re most confident; using real time polling, guess not the right answer but the answer that most people in the room think is correct. The twists will be different each time. We also rely heavily on audio and video clues to make the experience fun and engaging for everyone – even those that don’t like trivia. Interested? Gather your team of up to six players and compete for bragging rights!

21+. Free to attend/participate.
