Saturday May. 30th

CID Spring Clean

When

May 30th

9:00am - 12:00pm

Where

Hing Hay Park, Seattle WA 98104

Get Directions
Buy Tickets

More Information

Please join us for the 25th annual C-ID Spring Clean event on Saturday, May 30th, between 9:00am - 12:00pm!

The C-ID Spring Clean is an annual event dedicated to giving back to the neighborhood. Volunteers will be participating in an variety of beautification activities to help clean across Chinatown, Japantown, and Little Saigon. Volunteers will receive a free event T-shirt, breakfast, lunch, special offers, and community service hours (if needed).
