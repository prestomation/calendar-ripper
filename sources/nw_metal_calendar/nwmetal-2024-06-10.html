NW METAL CALENDAR

 

Last updated on 6/7/24.

ANNOUNCEMENT: As a public service, we started this page many years ago to list upcoming metal shows in the Seattle area (and sometimes elsewhere in the Pacific Northwest) as we become aware of them.

WARNING: We get our information from press releases and web sites for the venues for the shows, but we can’t guarantee that what we find and post here will stay accurate as time passes, so check it out for yourself. We don’t promise to update the info if a show is canceled or a band drops off a tour or the venue changes. So before you show up to buy tickets at the door or online, check with the venue to make sure nothing has changed.

CAVEAT: Sometimes our editor doesn’t list a show because he’s unfamiliar with the bands, or because he missed an announcement or forgot to check a venue’s calendar. If you’re a band or a venue and you want us to add your info here, or if you’re a music lover who thinks we ought to list an event you know of, leave a comment below or send an e-mail to: islander@nocleansinging.com
