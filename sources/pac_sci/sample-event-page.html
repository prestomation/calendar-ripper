Science After Dark

February 14, 7:00 p.m. - 10:00 p.m.
Buy Tickets

Celebrate Valentines Day at PacSci, whether you’re single, coupled up, or somewhere in between! Experience a Live Comedy Dating Show; explore the Tropical Butterfly House; get a tarot reading; and mingle with new faces at the Anti-Freeze Mixer!

What to Expect

  • Full access to 70+ exhibits
  • Hundreds of science lovers ready to mix, mingle, and make memories
  • Mating and Dating: Learn about love, sex, mating and relationships in the animal kingdom with Woodland Park Zoos Janel Kempf
  • Web of Desire: Learn all about spiders and watch them being fed with Hannah Held, PacScis Living Exhibit Manager
  • Paint and Sip: Grab a canvas, paint a friend, and create a masterpiece to take home.
  • Free Tarot readings with Primal Mystic Tarot and Susanna Cagle of Attune Yoga
  • Free Caricature Art by Catch the Sunshine: What do you look like as a flower?
  • Discounts on evening laser shows

Fun & Flirty Comedy Show: PNWs favorite interactive hybrid comedy/dating show that brings together love laughter, and this time science! Hosted by comedians Zahnae Aquino and Jesse Warren, Fun & Flirty explores the essence of dating in Seattle by presenting local singles with the help of the citys best comedians and a PowerPoint presentation highlighting all the very best attributes of the featured single hoping to find romance. There is a cast of hot, sexy singles that are pitched to the audience by comedians and also live speed dates consisting of audience members.

The Anti Freeze Mixer: Hosted by the Seattle Social Club
Looking to meet friends or more? Swing by the Anti Freeze Mixer which will have a variety of activities led by the Seattle Social Club.

Land Acknowledgment

Pacific Science Center acknowledges that we live, work, and play on the traditional territories of the Coast Salish Peoples and that we occupy this land. This acknowledgment does not take the place of authentic relationships with Indigenous communities but serves as a first step in honoring the land we occupy.

Commitment to Anti-Racism

Pacific Science Center stands with those who are courageously fighting against hate, racism, and injustice. We recognize that not being racist is not sufficient. We must be actively anti-racist, and we commit to doing so. We prioritize inclusion, diversity, equity, and access in everything we do. Curiosity and critical thinking are essential to the dialogue and changes that need to happen.
