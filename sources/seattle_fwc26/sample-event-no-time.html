Glassblowing is a global art form shaped by artists and techniques from all over the world, just like the teams taking the pitch. Join the Seattle Glassblowing Studio as we unite the local glass art with the global stage for a series of special events.

‍

June 1 through July 31st

‍

What’s Happening in our Hot Shop:

- Free glassblowing demonstrations every day.

- Handcrafted Shop: browse our collection of limited-edition soccer-inspired souvenirs.

- Create Your Own Glass Memory: make a commemorative token or create a piece in your team colors. Includes an option for soccer stamp.

‍

Take part in glassblowing to create a Seattle memory of the tournament. Hands-on bookings available the day before every match, or choose you own date.
